About me ✿

Hello! I'm so glad you stopped by. I started this blog because I wanted a small, quiet space to share the everyday things that bring me joy — the changing of the seasons, a good cup of tea, little discoveries, and the slow work of growing into yourself.

The name comes from the crocus — one of the first flowers of spring. It blooms even through the last frost, small and determined and quietly beautiful. That felt right.

This isn't a blog with a grand theme or a perfect aesthetic. It's simply a place to write, to remember, and to connect. If something here resonates with you, I'd love to hear from you.
