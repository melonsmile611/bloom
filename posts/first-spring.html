A First Hello from the Garden

There is something quietly brave about the crocus. It blooms in late winter — sometimes pushing up right through snow — long before any other flower dares. It doesn't wait for the perfect moment. It simply grows.

I've been thinking about that a lot lately, and it's part of why I wanted to start writing here. I'm not waiting until my life is more interesting, or my thoughts are more polished, or I have something important to say. I just want to begin. Quietly, like the crocus.

"It's not our job to stay small so others are comfortable."

This blog will be a mix of whatever my life holds — walks I go on, things I cook, books I'm reading, little moments that catch the light in a way I want to remember. Nothing grand. Just real.

If you've found your way here: hello. I'm glad you're here. I hope something in these pages makes you feel a little less alone, or a little more seen. That's all I could ask.
