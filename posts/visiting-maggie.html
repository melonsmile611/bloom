🏠 去 Maggie 家 | Visiting Maggie

May 20, 2026 Annie Baby minimalist prose

One Year Later

It's been more than a year.

Last time I was at Maggie's, she had just moved in. The house was empty. Nothing but bare walls and floors.

Today I went back. The stove was burning. Two sofas.

One leather three-seater. One fabric sofa, floral pattern, very beautiful.

Wicker chairs arranged in the middle.

Perfect for Bible study. Perfect for gatherings.

Ling Ling accompanied me. It was four o'clock.

My stomach was a little hungry. Ling Ling looked hungry too.

I worried we might not hold on. But it was fine. Not a problem at all.

Many Things to Share

It's been so long. Maggie had many things to talk about.

Talked about recent changes.

I really like how she arranges her home. Her aesthetic is excellent.

She bought a lot of second-hand furniture. But it's beautiful.

Good quality. The tables and such are very nice.

I love the things she淘s (finds).

She finally realized her idea. Years ago she wanted to open her home to others. Let people gather together.

Maybe not many people come. But her home is open now. This step has been taken.

Slow and Steady

Our wishes are achieved bit by bit, step by step.

God's plan is always beyond our understanding.

Just follow.

My rhythm has been too fast. I need to slow down.

Maggie needed to remind me. Sometimes life moves too quickly.

Ling Ling went with me today. It wasn't sudden. It felt arranged.

I was moved.

I really need this kind of friend to guide me.

Tired but Happy

I went to bed late again today.

Making videos lately has been exhausting. But exhausted and happy.

What are we doing, living?

Er Niu Goes to Canberra

Er Niu is going to Canberra tomorrow.

To be honest, I'm a bit worried.

In a few hours—three hours, maybe—she will leave.

I won't be able to get up to see her off.

Her father finally decided to return. At the last moment, he will rush to send her off tomorrow.

It's not easy.

I'm grateful. Thank you for your children.

He went to Launceston to study today. Came back in the afternoon.

Must have been very tired.

Recording Life Too Rushed

Lately recording life has been too hurried.

Every day I have endless work.

Today I was fiddling with my stickers again. Found so many.

They're done. But I don't know how to list them.

There are too many.

Touched to Tears

Before I prayed for her, she cried.

She said she had intentionally distanced herself from me for a while. She really couldn't watch anymore.

I understand his feelings.

My mom couldn't handle it back then either. She was on the verge of collapse.

I truly looked terrible. When I went for the medical test, they thought I was sixty or seventy years old.

I felt the same way. I dared not look in the mirror. I didn't want to for a long time.

I didn't really want to talk about these things. Afraid I would catch up with that state.

Isn't everyone like this? Going through it?

Who doesn't have bad times? Don't think too much.

Now I feel like my heart has a cocoon around it. A thick cocoon.

I don't want to touch what's inside.

Church

Again talking about going to church.

He said I should go to church.

I know I should go.

But I make excuses.

I don't think I'm ready.

I know I need to stop. But I can't stop.

If I stop, I'll think about too many things.

I don't want to think.

安静。

停。

走。

Written at night.
The day has ended. Quiet settles in.
Tomorrow, I will walk again.
